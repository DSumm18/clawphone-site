Getting Started

1. Download ClawPhone from the App Store

2. Get a connection code by messaging your Clawbot: "connect clawphone"

3. Enter the code in the app to link your AI

FAQ

Q: What is a Clawbot?

A: Clawbot is your personal AI assistant powered by OpenClaw. Get one at clawbot.ai

Q: Why isn't my voice being recognized?

A: Make sure you've granted microphone permissions. Go to Settings > ClawPhone > Microphone.

Q: How do I change voices?

A: Tap the gear icon in the chat screen to access voice settings.
