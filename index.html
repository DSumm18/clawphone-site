Frequently Asked Questions

What is Clawbot?

Clawbot is a personal AI assistant that runs on your own infrastructure - completely private and autonomous. Unlike ChatGPT, it can execute tasks, access your files, and maintain persistent memory. Get yours free at clawbot.ai.

Do I need a Clawbot to use ClawPhone?

Yes, ClawPhone is a voice interface for your Clawbot. You'll need to set up a Clawbot first (it's free and takes about 10 minutes), then connect it to ClawPhone.

How does the voice chat work?

You speak into your phone or watch, we convert it to text, send it to your Clawbot, get the response, and play it back in your chosen character voice. All in real-time!

Is my data private?

100%. Your messages go directly to YOUR Clawbot running on YOUR infrastructure. We never see, store, or process your conversations. The only thing we handle is the voice synthesis.

Can I request new voices?

Yes! We add new voices based on user requests. Let us know who you want to talk to and we'll try to add them in our monthly updates.
