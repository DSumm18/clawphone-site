ClawPhone Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

Overview

ClawPhone ("we", "our", or "the app") is a voice chat interface that connects to your personal AI assistant. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

Voice Data: Voice recordings are processed in real-time for speech-to-text conversion and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Messages: Text messages sent through the app are transmitted to your connected AI assistant. Message history may be stored locally on your device.

Device Identifier: A random device identifier is used to maintain your connection to your AI assistant. This is not linked to your personal identity.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Third-Party Services

Text-to-Speech: We use Fish Audio for voice synthesis. Voice output is generated based on your AI's responses. No personal data is shared with this service.

Your AI Assistant: Messages are sent to your personally configured AI assistant (Clawbot). The data handling for your AI is governed by your own configuration.

Data Security

All communications between the app and our servers use HTTPS encryption. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Children's Privacy

ClawPhone is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You can disconnect your AI assistant at any time from the app settings. Local message history can be cleared by reinstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: support@clawbot.ai
